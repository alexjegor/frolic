# Hydroponic Methods Comparison

## Nutrient Film Technique (NFT)

A thin layer of nutrient solution flows continuously across plant roots.

Structure:

tank
→ pump
→ channel with roots
→ drain
→ tank

Advantages:

- efficient nutrient usage
- good oxygen exposure for roots
- fast growth

Disadvantages:

- roots may block channels
- pump failure can quickly damage plants

Common failure mode: root mass clogging the flow channel.

---

## Flood and Drain (Ebb and Flow)

Grow trays periodically flood with water and then drain back into a reservoir.

Structure:

reservoir
→ pump
→ grow tray
→ drain
→ reservoir

Cycle:

pump ON → tray fills
pump OFF → tray drains

Advantages:

- strong root aeration
- simple mechanics
- resistant to clogging

Disadvantages:

- requires timer/controller
- pump cycles frequently

---

## Drip Irrigation

Each plant receives water through a drip emitter.

Structure:

tank
→ pump
→ manifold
→ drip emitters

Advantages:

- precise watering
- scalable

Disadvantages:

- emitters clog easily
- requires filtration

---

## Kratky Method (Passive Hydroponics)

A passive system without pumps.

Structure:

plant above reservoir
roots grow into water
air gap forms over time

Advantages:

- no electricity required
- extremely simple
- minimal hardware

Disadvantages:

- limited control
- works only for certain plants

---

## Aeroponics

Roots are suspended in air and sprayed with nutrient mist.

Structure:

mist nozzle
→ roots suspended in air

Advantages:

- maximum oxygen exposure
- extremely fast growth

Disadvantages:

- high complexity
- spray nozzles clog easily

---

## Comparison Summary

| Method | Complexity | Clog Risk | Power Required | Growth Speed |
|--------|-----------|-----------|---------------|-------------|
| NFT | Medium | High (root blockage) | Yes (pump) | Fast |
| Flood & Drain | Low | Low | Yes (pump + timer) | Medium |
| Drip | Medium | High (emitters) | Yes (pump) | Medium |
| Kratky | Very Low | None | No | Slow |
| Aeroponics | High | High (nozzles) | Yes (pump + mist) | Very Fast |
