# Hydroponics + IoT + 3D Printing — Project Index

## Overview

Hydroponics is a method of growing plants **without soil**, where roots receive water and dissolved nutrients directly from a water solution.

Goals:
- Indoor cultivation with efficient water usage
- IoT-based monitoring and automation (ESP32)
- 3D printed structural components
- Simple, maintainable plumbing design

Monitored parameters:
- temperature
- humidity
- water level
- pH
- nutrient concentration (EC/TDS)
