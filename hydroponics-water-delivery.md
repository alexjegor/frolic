# Water Delivery & Plumbing

## Pump Driven Systems

reservoir
→ pump
→ distribution system
→ plants
→ return line
→ reservoir

Advantages:

- stable pressure
- uniform distribution
- easy automation

Disadvantages:

- pump dependency
- higher energy consumption

---

## Gravity Systems

Water is supplied from a higher reservoir and flows downward naturally.

tank above system
→ gravity flow
→ plants
→ drainage

Advantages:

- low energy use
- fewer moving parts

Disadvantages:

- uneven water distribution
- sensitive to blockages
- harder to control flow
